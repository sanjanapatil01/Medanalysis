About-Us

Health Risk Predictor is an AI-driven platform designed specifically for doctors to enhance decision-making, improve patient outcomes, and streamline clinical workflows. Our mission is to empower healthcare professionals with cutting-edge tools that predict hospital readmissions, optimize medication plans, and assess chronic disease risks using Electronic Health Records (EHR) data.

By leveraging advanced machine learning algorithms, we help identify high-risk patients early, personalized and evidence-based treatment strategies , and reduce unnecessary readmissions . Our platform integrates effortlessly with existing EHR systems, ensuring a seamless user experience without disrupting current practices.

We Serve

At Health Risk Predictor, we specialize in early detection and risk stratification for Cardiovascular Disease (CVD) and Diabetes, two of the most prevalent and high-impact chronic conditions.

Cardiovascular Disease (CVD)

Cardiovascular Disease (CVD) refers to a group of disorders that affect the heart and blood vessels. Common types include coronary artery disease, heart failure, stroke, and hypertension (high blood pressure).

Diabetes

Diabetes is a chronic condition where the body cannot properly regulate blood sugar (glucose) levels. It is mainly categorized into Type 1 (autoimmune) and Type 2 (linked to insulin resistance and lifestyle).
